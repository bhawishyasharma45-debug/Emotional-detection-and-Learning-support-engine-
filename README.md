
# AI-Driven Emotion Detection & Personalized Learning Support Platform

## Introduction

The **AI-Driven Emotion Detection & Personalized Learning Support Platform** is an end-to-end AI application designed to understand students' emotions from their study-related text and provide personalized learning support. The platform combines **BiLSTM** and **BERT**-based emotion detection models with **rule-based keyword enhancement** and **Google Gemini AI** to generate empathetic, context-aware guidance for learners.

Users can describe their study challenges in natural language, and the system automatically detects emotions such as **Bored, Confident, Confused, Curious,** and **Frustrated**. Based on the detected emotion, the platform provides personalized suggestions, learning strategies, and motivational support to help students continue their learning journey with confidence.

In addition to emotion detection, the platform records user interactions in a CSV file for future analysis, visualizes emotion trends using an interactive analytics dashboard, and supports side-by-side comparison between the **BiLSTM** and **BERT** models. This creates a transparent and efficient workflow that delivers fast, consistent, and supportive responses while helping educators and learners better understand learning emotions over time.

---

## Scenario 1: Educators, Teaching Assistants, and Academic Support Teams

Educators and academic support staff often spend significant time reading student messages and trying to understand their emotional state before providing assistance. This manual process can delay responses and may overlook students who need immediate support.

The AI Learning Assistant simplifies this process by automatically analyzing student text, identifying emotions, displaying confidence scores, and generating supportive, subject-aware responses. For example, a teaching assistant can paste a student's message into the application and instantly determine whether the student is **Confused**, **Frustrated**, or experiencing a combination of emotions. The platform also compares predictions from both **BiLSTM** and **BERT**, improving transparency and helping educators make better-informed decisions. As a result, instructors can spend more time supporting students instead of manually interpreting emotions.

---

## Scenario 2: Learners and Self-Study

Students learning independently often face difficulties when they become stuck, confused, or frustrated. Searching for solutions across multiple websites or waiting for assistance can interrupt the learning process.

The AI Learning Assistant allows learners to simply describe their study problem in their own words and immediately receive emotion-aware guidance and practical next steps. For example, if a student writes, *"I'm lost on recursion,"* the platform identifies the emotion as **Confused**, provides encouraging feedback, and suggests effective learning strategies to improve understanding.

The system can also detect **mixed emotions**, such as **Curious + Confused**, allowing learners to better understand their own learning experience. Every interaction is securely logged to build a history of progress, enabling users to reflect on their learning journey over time. This makes the platform a valuable companion for self-paced learning, academic support, and continuous skill development.
